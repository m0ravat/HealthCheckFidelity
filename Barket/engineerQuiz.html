Instructions

To complete a health check, you'll go through a series of statements related to key aspects of the team's performance and well-being.
For each statement, you'll rate it using a button that corresponds to Amber, Red, or Green, based on the current state of that area.
Each color is also associated with a trend indicator—Stable, Improving, or Worsening—to track progress over time.
This helps identify strengths and areas needing improvement, ensuring a clear overview of the team's overall health.
